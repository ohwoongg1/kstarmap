CJ아지트 광흥창

In Episode 16 (the final episode), this is the location where the concert of Mush & Co. and Crude Play takes place.

14 Changjeon-ro, Mapo-gu, Seoul

뚝섬한강공원

In Episode 1, Yoon So-rim (Joy) falls for Kang Han-gyeol (Lee Hyun-woo) at first sight in front of this complex. In Episode 2, she returns to this location with her band, hoping to meet him again.

139 Gangbyeon Buk-ro, Gwangjin-gu, Seoul

롯데몰 은평점

In Episode 2, Kang Han-gyeol (Lee Hyun-woo) and Yoon Su-rim (Joy) met at this shopping mall.

1050 Tongil-ro, Eunpyeong-gu, Seoul

마로니에 공원

The café setting from Episode 5 where Yoon Su-rim (Joy) shared the good news with Kang Han-gyeol (Lee Hyun-woo) about being scouted by Solo N Music Agency.

104 Daehak-ro, Jongno-gu, Seoul

한강대교

In Episodes 9 and 10, Yoon Su-rim (Park Su-young) chases after Kang Han-gyeol (Lee Hyun-woo) after the Mushand Co. debut showcase, and Han-gyeol confesses his feelings for her on this bridge.

Hangang Daegyo, Ichon-dong, Yongsan-gu, Seoul
