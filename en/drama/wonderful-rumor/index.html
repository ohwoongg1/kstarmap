동화컬처빌리지

In Episode 4, Mayor Shin Myung-hee (Choi Kwang-il) announces the future development direction of Jeongjin City to the media at this location.

1708-38 Bukhangang-ro, Hwado-eup, Namyangju-si, Gyeonggi-do

로케이션 이음 (구 로얄퀘스트)

The office of Jeon Gi-hwan, former chairman of Tae Shin Group (played by Lee Do-kyung), who is murdered by a demon appearing in the drama. This location frequently appears in Episodes 4-6.

48-70 Saeo-ri-ro 286beon-gil, Tanhyeon-myeon, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do

쉴낙원 김포장례식장

In Episode 1, it appears at the funeral of the late Jang Cheol-jeong (played by Sung Ji-roo), where CEO Choi Jang-mul (played by Ahn Seok-hwan) pays his respects.

7 Seungga-ro 58beon-gil, Gimpo-si, Gyeonggi-do

쌍대포소금구이 본점

In Episode 11, Gamo Tak (Yoo Joon-sang) recalls a moment from seven years ago when Detective So Kwon (Jeon Seok-ho) sat at his table in this restaurant and told him about the relationship between Mayor Shin and Kim Young...

291 Cheongpa-ro, Yongsan-gu, Seoul

용현1.4동

In Episode 1, close friends Rumor (Jo Byung-kyoo), Lim Ju-yeon (Lee Ji-won), and Kim Woong-min (Kim Eun-soo) pass by this house on their way to school.

81-22 Subongnam-ro 18beon-gil, Michuhol-gu, Incheon

운산

In Episode 7, Mayor Shin Myung-hwi (Choi Gwang-il) and the representative (Kim Gwi-seon) meet at this restaurant.

101 Yeouigongwon-ro, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul
