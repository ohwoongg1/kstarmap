성산대교

In Episode 4, Seo Yi-gyeong (Lee Yo-won) and Lee Se-jin (Uee) come under this bridge to have a conversation.

Seongsandaegyo, Mangwon-dong, Mapo-gu, Seoul

루쏘소 분당판교점

In Episode 12, Seo Gyeong (Lee Yo-won) and Lee Se-jin (YUI) meet and play a game. Everything hinges on whether Gyeong will accept the check. Afterwards, she joins forces with Park Gun-woo (Jin Gu) to defend Se-jin.

1 Pangyo-yeok-ro 2-beon-gil, Bundang-gu, Seongnam-si, Gyeonggi-do, 1st Floor

세븐일레븐 평창점

In Episode 7, Lee Se-jin (Yui) is working part-time after being fired from Gallery S.

17 Pyeongchang 11-gil, Jongno-gu, Seoul

엘빈에비뉴라운지

In Episode 1, Seo Yi-kyung (Lee Yeo-won) plays Russian roulette with gangsters on the first floor.

13 Gangnam-daero 92-gil, Gangnam-gu, Seoul
