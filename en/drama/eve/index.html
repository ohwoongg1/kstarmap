인천국제공항

In Episode 1, the story flashes back to 2009, where Seo Eun-pyeong (Lee Sang-yeop) encounters a young Israel (Kim Ji-an) waiting for a flight to the United States at the airport.

272 Gonghang-ro, Jung-gu, Incheon

합천 영상 테마파크

In Episode 1, you can see Seo Eun-pyeong (played by Lee Sang-yeop) visiting the Blue House to have a direct conversation with the President.

757 Hapcheonhosoo-ro, Yongju-myeon, Hapcheon-gun, Gyeongsangnam-do
