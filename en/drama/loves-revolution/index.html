롯데월드

In Episode 14, Byun Hyuk (Choi Si-won), Baek Jun (Kang So-ra), and Kwon Jae-hun (Gong Myung) head to this amusement park.

240 Olympic-ro, Songpa-gu, Seoul

마로니에 공원

In a flashback scene in Episode 1, you can see what confession Baek Jun (Kang So-ra) made to Kwon Je-hun (Gong Myung).

104 Daehak-ro, Jongno-gu, Seoul

파라다이스시티

This is the location where the Kangsu Group celebration took place in Episode 1.

186 Yeongjonghaeansnam-ro 321beon-gil, Jung-gu, Incheon

화이트블럭

In Episode 14, everyone gathers at this location to plan their afternoon activities together.

72 Heyri-maul-gil, Tanhyeon-myeon, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do

노양진로 아파트

Baek Jun (Kang So-ra) lives in the rooftop apartment of this building. Kwon Je-hun (Gong Myung) lives in the basement of the apartment. Byeon Hyuk (Choi Si-won) will soon move in with Baek Jun.

78 Noryangijn-ro 32-gil, Dongjak-gu, Seoul

라비두스

In Episode 9, this building appears as Byun Hyuk's (Choi Si-won) family mansion.

7 Pildong-ro 5-gil, Jung-gu, Seoul

로케이션 이음 (구 로얄퀘스트)

The airplane scene related to Byun Hyuk (Choi Si-won)'s video scandal was filmed at this location.

48-70 Saeo-ri-ro 286beon-gil, Tanhyeon-myeon, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do

아셈타워

This is the headquarters of Kang Soo Group. In Episode 6, Byun Hyuk (Choi Si-won) is seen cleaning windows on the exterior of the building.

517 Yeongdong-daero, Gangnam-gu, Seoul

아소산 일산본점

In Episode 11, Manager Yang brings Byeon Hyuk (Choi Si-won) to this location for a business meeting with Director Min (Song Young-gyu), and Hyuk appears noticeably anxious.

128 Jeongbalsan-ro, Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do

윤희빌딩

Episode 7: The location where Min Dae-representative (Song Young-kyou) received notification that his service contract was terminated.

22-8 Sinchon-yeok-ro, Seodaemun-gu, Seoul

카페잔피(구 히스토리 인천점)

In Episode 2, on the way home. Hyun stops upon seeing a dandelion and blows away its seeds.

13-1 Songhak-ro, Jung-gu, Incheon

콘래드 서울 호텔

In Episode 1, Byun Hyuk (Choi Si-won) is swimming at a hotel pool. He spots his ex-girlfriend and chases after her, but loses his locker key. He quickly changes into a pool staff uniform and follows her.

10 Gukjegugeumyung-ro, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul
