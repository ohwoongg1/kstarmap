롯데월드

After quitting her job in Episode 8, Choi Su-ah (Kim Ha-neul) goes there with her daughter.

240 Olympic-ro, Songpa-gu, Seoul

반포대교

Episode 1: Seo Do-woo (Lee Sang-yoon) arrives at this location late at night to show his daughter.

Banpo-daegyo, Banpo-dong, Seocho-gu, Seoul

안성팜랜드

In Episode 4, Seo Do-woo (Lee Sang-yoon) visits this location while following traces of his deceased adopted daughter Annie (Park Seo-yeon). Annie had come here to find her biological father.

28 Daesindo-gil, Gongdo-eup, Anseong-si, Gyeonggi-do

우리옛돌박물관

In Episode 4, the road to the airport where Kim Hye-won (Jang Hee-jin) goes to make an appointment.

66 Daesagwan-ro 13-gil, Seongbuk-gu, Seoul

인천국제공항

This is the workshop of Choi Su-ah (Kim Ha-neul) and Park Jin-sook (Shin Sung-rok).

272 Gonghang-ro, Jung-gu, Incheon

화이트블럭

In Episode 16, after Sua breaks up with her husband, this is also where Choi Sua (Kim Ha-neul) and Seo Do-woo (Lee Sang-yoon) meet at 2 PM. At that moment, they decide to maintain their relationship through text messages...

72 Heyri-maul-gil, Tanhyeon-myeon, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do

낙천정 나들목

In Episode 3, the protagonist Kim Ha-neul frequently takes calls here from Seo Do-woo (Lee Sang-yoon).

576-1 Jayang 2-dong, Gwangjin-gu, Seoul

신창풍차해안 등대

In Episode 10, Choi Su-ah (Kim Ha-neul) arrives in Jeju Island with her daughter and searches for a lighthouse.

1481-14 Sinchang-ri, Hangyeong-myeon, Jeju-si, Jeju Special Self-Governing Province

용인공원

In Episodes 3-4, this place becomes a sanctuary for Ani (Park Seo-yeon), and Seo-do (Lee Sang-yoon) comes looking for her.

158-33 Sarae-ro, Mohyeon-eup, Chein-gu, Yongin-si, Gyeonggi-do

이태원동 고가도로

Choi Soo-ah (Kim Ha-neul) and Park Jin-sook (Shin Sung-rok) go on a drive.

488 Itaewon-dong, Yongsan-gu, Seoul

일비앙코

In Episodes 10-11, this is the location where Choi Soo-ah (played by Kim Ha-neul) meets her husband and discusses moving. As she leaves, Seo Do-woo (played by Lee Sang-yoon) coincidentally enters the same place.

189 Seongbok 1-ro, Suji-gu, Yongin-si, Gyeonggi-do

장욱진미술문화재단

This is the workplace of Go Eun-hee (Ye Soo-jung), Seo Do-woo (Lee Sang-yoon), and his wife Hye-won (Jang Hee-jin).

119-8 Mabuk-ro, Giheung-gu, Yongin-si, Gyeonggi-do

종달초등학교

A school in Jeju Island that Park Hyo-eun (Kim Hwan-hee), the daughter of Choi Su-ah (Kim Ha-neul), attends.

91-21 Yaksangol-gil, Tanhyeon-myeon, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do

최수아의 집

This is the house where Choi Su-ah (Kim Ha-neul) and her daughter live in Jeju Island.

5 Jongdal-ro 7-gil, Guza-eup, Jeju-si, Jeju Special Self-Governing Province

한울랜드

In Episode 10, Choi Su-ah (played by Kim Ha-neul) arrives in Jeju Island with her daughter and heads to this location.

671 Seohaeoan-ro, Jeju-si, Jeju Special Self-Governing Province
