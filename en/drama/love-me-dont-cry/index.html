망원한강공원

Heartbroken Han Young-min (played by Lee Jung-jin) drives to this park in Episode 50, where he gets into an accident near the riverbank.

467 Maponaeru-gil, Mapo-gu, Seoul

선유도공원

In Episode 15, after having tea and conversing with Han Young-min (played by Lee Jung-jin), Jo Mi-su (played by Lee Yu-ri) comes to this park and reunites with Jang Hyun-woo (played by Lee Sang-yoon).

343 Seonyu-ro, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

양화한강공원

In Episode 2, Jang Hyun-woo (Lee Sang-yoon) arrives to pick up Jo Mi-soo (Lee Yu-ri) in his mother's impressive sports car.

221 Nodeul-ro, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

인천국제공항

In Episode 132 (the final episode), Jang Hyun-woo (Lee Sang-yoon) leaves this place.

272 Gonghang-ro, Jung-gu, Incheon

MBC일산드림센터

In Episode 36, Jo Mi-su (Lee Yu-ri) invites Han Young-min (Lee Jung-jin) to a Seoul Philharmonic Orchestra performance held at this building.

596 Hosu-ro, Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do

무궁화로

Jo Mi-su (Lee Yu-ri) and Han Young-min (Lee Jung-jin) meet by chance.

Mungunghwa-ro, Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do

영동대로

In Episode 31, after Jang Hyun-woo (Lee Sang-yoon) and Jo Mi-su (Lee Yu-ri) visit a café with their mother, they drive through this street.

Samseong-dong, Gangnam-gu, Seoul

이랜드크루즈

In Episode 39, after Han Young-min (Lee Jung-jin) realizes that he and his lover cannot be together, he takes Jo Mi-su (Lee Yu-ri) to Elph Restaurant (now closed).

280 Yeouido-ro, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

잠실롯데캐슬골드

Min Seo-yeong (Oh Seung-hyun), the fiancée of Han Young-min (Lee Jung-jin), lives in an apartment in this complex.

269 Olympic-ro, Songpa-gu, Seoul

장호빌딩

Han Young-min (Lee Jung-jin) and Jo Mi-su (Lee Yu-ri) meet at this café.

27 Wausan-ro 19-gil, Mapo-gu, Seoul

푸르지오 써밋

There is a model of this building in Han Young-min's (Lee Jung-jin) room.

337 Yeongdong-daero, Gangnam-gu, Seoul
