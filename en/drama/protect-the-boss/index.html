63스퀘어

In Episode 2, an anniversary celebration takes place at this ballroom. Cha Ji-heon (Ji Sung) introduces himself as the new Chief Financial Officer until he has to leave the room due to a panic attack.

50 63-ro, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

무궁화로

In Episode 18 (the final episode), Chairman Cha Bong-man (played by Park Young-gyu) is doing community service at the crosswalk on this street. There, he pleads with Noh Eun-seol (played by Choi Kang-hee) and Cha Ji-heon...

Mungunghwa-ro, Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do

쌍대포소금구이 본점

In Episode 1, Kim Secretary (Kim Hyung-beom) drinks at this location and overhears a reporter's conversation, leading to a new scandal surrounding Cha Ji-hoon (Ji-hoon) being reported in the media.

291 Cheongpa-ro, Yongsan-gu, Seoul

오보코갤러리

In Episodes 2 and 18, Cha Ji-heon (Ji Sung) lives here with his father, Chairman Cha Bong.

15 Seonjam-ro 5-gil, Seongbuk-gu, Seoul

한길 북하우스

In Episode 7, Cha Ji-heon (Ji Sung) and Cha Mu-won (Kim Jae-joong) meet at this location.

59-6 Heyri-maeul-gil, Tanhyeon-myeon, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do
