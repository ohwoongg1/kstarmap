동탄호수공원

In the drama, Sim Eun-seok (Kim Hye-soo) is frequently shown jogging in this park. See Episode 1.

라베니체 마치에비뉴

In Episodes 2 and 4 of Juvenile Justice, Judge Shim Eun-seok (Kim Hye-soo) and Cha Tae-joo (Kim Moo-yeol) are drinking at a pojangmacha (tent bar) located across from Building 1 of this location.

12 Gimpo Hangang 4-ro, Gimpo-si, Gyeonggi-do
