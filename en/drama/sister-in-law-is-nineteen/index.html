All Locations

성산대교

In Episode 10, Kang Seung-jae (Yoon Kye-sang) and Choi Su-ji (Kim Min-hee) ask Han Yu-min (Jung Da-bin) under the bridge why she loves her so much.

Seongsandaegyo, Mangwon-dong, Mapo-gu, Seoul

원효대교

In Episode 11, everyone celebrates Yumin's birthday at this location, eating cake and setting off fireworks.

Wonhyo-daegyo, Yeouido-dong, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

의정부 예술의전당

In Episode 11, Kang Seung-jae (Yoon Gae-sang) gives performance tickets to Han Yu-min (Jung Da-bin) and his younger brother Kang Min-jae (Kim Jae-won) at this concert venue.

1 Uijeong-ro, Uijeongbu-si, Gyeonggi-do

중앙고등학교

Kang Seung-jae (Yoon Gye-sang) attends this high school. Han Yu-min (Jung Da-bin) joins in Episode 4.

164 Changdeokgung-gil, Jongno-gu, Seoul

국민건강보험공단 일산병원

This is the workplace where Kang Min-jae (Kim Jae-won) works as a doctor in the drama. It is also where Yoo-min's (Jung Da-bin) younger brother Han Gang-pyo (Heo Jung-min) receives treatment.

100 Ilsan-ro, Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do

밀레니엄 힐튼 서울

Kang Min-jae discovers Han Yu-min on the hotel staircase. Upon learning that she has been hurt by Kang Seung-jae's past remarks, he sits beside her and offers her comfort.

50 Sowol-ro, Jung-gu, Seoul

삐노꼴레

All important meetings take place here. For example, in Episodes 2-3, there's a scene where Kang Min-jae makes a contract proposal to Han Yu-min, and she comes here for dinner with her father.

1077 Gwangneung Sukmogwon-ro, Soheul-eup, Pocheon-si, Gyeonggi-do

서강대교

Kang Min-jae comes to this place and finally gathers the courage to call out to his long-time crush.

Seogang Daegyo, Yeouido-dong, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul
