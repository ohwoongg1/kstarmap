성산대교

Episode 4: On this bridge, he attempts to persuade Yang Ok-kyung (Gyeon Mi-ri) about his love for Kang Se-mi (An Mi-na).

Seongsandaegyo, Mangwon-dong, Mapo-gu, Seoul

경희대학교 서울캠퍼스

In Episode 1, Kang Jun-woo (Song Chang-ui) reminisces about happy moments from his school days with Ok Ji-young (Choi Yeo-jin).

26 Gyeonghui-daero, Dongdaemun-gu, Seoul

그랜드 워커힐 서울

In Episode 5, Ok Ji-young (Choi Yeo-jin) and Kim Young-min (Song Jong-ho) celebrate their engagement at this house.

177 Walkerhill-ro, Gwangjin-gu, Seoul

대한다원보성녹차밭

In the final scene of Episode 64, a happy family—Kang Jun-woo (Song Chang-ui), Nguyen Jinu (Lee Young-ah), and their child—walks through this tea field.

763-43 Nokcha-ro, Boseong-eup, Boseong-gun, Jeollanam-do

르한스

When Ok-kyung begins to brag about her son's strengths, Han-sook receives a phone call and suddenly leaves in a hurry.

127 Jeongneung-ro 10-gil, Seongbuk-gu, Seoul

서울랜드

In Episode 5, Kim Myung-soo (Kim Hee-chul) brings Kang Se-mi (Ahn Mi-na), the object of his unrequited love, to this park under the pretense of meeting her. However, she quickly sees through his intentions and turns away...

181 Gwangmyeong-ro, Gwacheon-si, Gyeonggi-do

양평 두물머리

In Episode 4, Kang Woo-nam (Kang Shin-il) brings Kang Jun-woo (Song Chang-ui) and Jin Joo Nguyen (Lee Young-ah) to this location the day before the arranged marriage.

Yangssu-ri, Yangseo-myeon, Yangpyeong-gun, Gyeonggi-do
