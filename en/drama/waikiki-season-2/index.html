벽초지수목원

In Episode 1, Gi-bong (Shin Hyun) and Lee Jun-gi (Lee I-kyung) walk through this park while finding their way back home.

242 Buheung-ro, Gwangtan-myeon, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do

중앙고등학교

As shown in Episode 1, this high school is where Cha Woo-sik (Kim Sun-ho), Lee Jun-ki (Lee I-kyung), Kook Ki-bong (Shin Hyun-soo), and Han Soo-yeon (Moon Ga-young) attend.

164 Changdeokgung-gil, Jongno-gu, Seoul

C156

In Episode 5, Cha U-sik (Kim Sun-ho) is shown going on a date with Da-young (Park A-in).

156 Dongil-ro, Gwangjin-gu, Seoul

경의선숲길공원

In this story, Cha Woo-sik (Kim Sun-ho) goes on a date with the landlord of the guesthouse Waikiki (Jeon Su-kyung) and realizes that their feelings cannot be undone.

80 Yeonnam-ro, Mapo-gu, Seoul
