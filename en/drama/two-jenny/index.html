All Locations

물빛광장(여의도)

In Episode 2, Park Jung-min (played by Kim Sung-chul) attempts a public busking performance at this location.

84-9 Yeouido-dong, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

신성리 갈대밭

In Episode 2, this is one of the places visited during a countryside trip by Kwon Nara/Jenny (Jung Chae-yeon) and Park Jung-min (Kim Seung-chul).

125-1 Sinseong-ri, Hansan-myeon, Seocheon-gun, Chungcheongnam-do

CU 부암럭키점

A convenience store where Park Jung-min (Kim Seung-chul) works part-time.

15 Baekseokdong-gil, Jongno-gu, Seoul

WM 엔터테인먼트

In Episode 2, the exterior of 'JW Entertainment' building appears, where Kwon Nara/Jenny (Jung Chae-yeon) spent 7 years as a trainee.

8 World Cup-ro 15-gil, Mapo-gu, Seoul

서울공연예술고등학교

Park Jung-min (played by Kim Sung-chul) and Kwon Na-ra/Jenny (played by Jung Chae-yeon) attended high school at 'Mirae High School'.

16-26 Ori-ro 22na-gil, Guro-gu, Seoul

서천 마량포구

Episode 2: The rural village where Nara/Jenny (Jung Chae-yeon) takes Jeong-min (Kim Sung-chul)

344 Maryang-ri, Seomyeon, Seocheon-gun, Chungcheongnam-do

세녹

The location where Kwon Nara/Jenny (Jung Chae-yeon) works part-time.

304 Sadang-ro, Dongjak-gu, Seoul

언플러그드

Visible in multiple scenes throughout Episode 2.

26 Wauwsan-ro 33-gil, Mapo-gu, Seoul

자하문로

This street appears in multiple scenes throughout the series. For example, in Episode 1, Jeong-min (Kim Sung-chul) passes by a mural on this street.

223-1 Jahamun-ro, Jongno-gu, Seoul
