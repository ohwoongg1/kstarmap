경희대국제캠퍼스

In Episode 1, Kim Jung-ho (played by Lee Seung-gi) and Kim Yu-ri (played by Lee Se-young) are students at this university. Yu-ri confesses her feelings to Jung-ho along the cherry blossom path that lines this plaza.

1732 Deokyeong-daero, Giheung-gu, Yongin-si, Gyeonggi-do

서울시티클럽

In Episode 4, Kim Jung-ho (Lee Seung-gi) is deceived by his father (whom he has severed ties with) and meets him at this restaurant. After several encounters, he leaves.

101 Yeouigongwon-ro, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul, C.C.M.M Building

아소산 일산본점

In Episode 11, Yuri Kim (Lee Se-young) and Jeong-ho Kim (Lee Seung-gi) met Han Representative (Kim Won-hae) at this restaurant to get a lead on the Dohan Group.

128 Jeongbalsan-ro, Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do

파크원

In Episode 16, Kim Yuri (Lee Se-young) goes shopping with her best friend Han Seo-yeon (Kim Seul-gi) and Kim Jung-ho's mother Lee Yeon-ju (Lee Mi-sook), which makes her reconsider their divorce plans.

108 Yeouidae-ro, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

퍼스트가든
