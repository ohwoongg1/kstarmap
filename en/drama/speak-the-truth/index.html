라페스타

At this location, detectives Yang Man-tu (Ryu Seung-soo), Jang Tae-sung (Shin Su-ho), and police officer Cha Su-young (Suzy) track down the man who previously attacked profiler Na Jun-seok (Song Young-gyu).

1305-56 Jungang-ro, Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do

연경 차이나타운점

In Episode 8, a photographer is filming the area while climbing the stairs next to this restaurant.

41 Chinatownro, Jung-gu, Incheon

용마랜드

In Episodes 7-8, 'Maze Land' is an abandoned theme park that was operated by lawyer Kim Na-hee's late father, serves as her residence, and is where young Gil Hyun-soo is being held captive.

118 Mangwu-ro 70-gil, Jungnang-gu, Seoul

차이나타운로44번길

In Episodes 8, 9, and 11, the series depicts Haewol-dong, a neighborhood in the fictional village of Muchon, which is portrayed as gang territory claimed by various factions.

44 Chinatownro-gil, Jung-gu, Incheon

회현시민아파트

In Episode 3, Police Officer Cha Su-young (Girls' Generation's Sooyoung) returns to the house of serial killer Kang Seung-hwan located in this building to check for evidence she may have missed.

101 Toegyero 8-gil, Jung-gu, Seoul
