원효대교

In Episode 22, Seong Hae-sung (Yeo Jin-gu) and Cha-ssi (Ahn Jae-hyun) resign from SMT Restaurant and start a new business venture. They first open a tea pasta food truck beneath this bridge.

Wonhyo-daegyo, Yeouido-dong, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

SMT HOUSE 차이나룸 청담점

Jung Jung-won (Lee Yeon-hee) appears at 'SNT,' an Italian restaurant run by Cha Shi (Ahn Jae-hyun), where she works as a sous chef.

58 Apgujeong-ro 79-gil, Gangnam-gu, Seoul

경의선숲길공원

In Episode 25, Sehee gives Jiho a pair of earrings as a gift.

80 Yeonnam-ro, Mapo-gu, Seoul

서울특별시 보라매병원

Seong Su-ji (played by Kim Hye-jun) is admitted to this hospital, which appears as 'Taesan Medical Center' in Episode 36, and Seong Young-in (played by Kim Ga-eun) learns about her illness.

20 Boramae-ro 5-gil, Dongjak-gu, Seoul

아트갤러리카페

In Episode 18, Cha Tae-hoon (Kim Jin-woo) had a blind date at this café with Lee Seo-won (Han So-hee), who had just quit working at the café.

445-2 Hoedong-gil, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do

퍼스트가든

In Episode 18, Sung Hae-sung (Yeo Jin-goo) takes Jung-won (Lee Yeon-hee) to the park.

260 Tapsakgol-gil, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do

회현동 맨션

Jeong Jung-won (Lee Yeon-hee) and Hong Jin-joo (Park Jin-joo) are living together.

36-2 Toegyero 6-ga-gil, Jung-gu, Seoul
