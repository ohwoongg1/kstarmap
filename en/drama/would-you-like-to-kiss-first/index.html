낙산공원

In Episode 1, Son Moo-han (Gam Woo-sung) comes here with his dog to think creatively.

41 Naksan-gil, Jongno-gu, Seoul

서울대공원

In Episode 33, Son Moo-han (Gam Woo-sung) and Ahn Soon-jin (Kim Sun-a) visit the Rose Garden Children's Zoo.

102 Daegongwon-gwangjang-ro, Gwacheon-si, Gyeonggi-do

경기영어마을 파주캠퍼스

In Episode 14, Lee Su (Ong Seong-wu) calls Jung Woo-yeon (Shin Ye-eun) at night from a location in the United States during a photo trip abroad.

40 Eolmumsil-ro, Tanhyeon-myeon, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do

북촌로5나길

In Episode 12, Son Muhan (Kam Woo-sung) and Ahn Sun-jin (Kim Sun-ah) go on a date and walk down this street.

3-15 Bukchon-ro 5-na-gil, Jongno-gu, Seoul

서울중앙지방법원

In Episode 4's flashback scene, Son Moo-han (Gam Woo-sung) witnesses a conversation between Ahn Sun-jin (Kim Sun-a) and Eun Kyung-su (Oh Ji-ho).

157 Seocho Jungang-ro, Seocho-gu, Seoul

일산문화광장

Eun Gyeong-su (Oh Ji-ho) passes by this park while on a phone call with Baek Ji-min (Park Si-yeon) in Episode 10 of 2018.

1271-4 Jungang-ro, Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do

카페 릴리블랑

Appears as a café in Episodes 1, 3, and 9.

52 Yangjaecheon-ro 19-gil, Seocho-gu, Seoul
