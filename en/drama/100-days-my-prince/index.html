광한루원

In the drama, the main characters meet and have conversations in the palace garden settings (particularly Gwanghallus, Ojak Bridge, and Wanyuejeong).

1447 Yocheon-ro, Namwon-si, Jeollabuk-do

아홉산숲

In Episode 6, Yeon Hong-shim/Yoon I-seo (Nam Ji-hyun) accepts a request through 'Solution Agency' to play the role of a client's girlfriend for Lee Yul/Na Won-deuk (Do Kyung-soo/EXO D.O).

520-10 Ungcheon-ri, Cheolma-myeon, Gijang-gun, Busan

영주 무섬마을

This village appears as the hometown where Yeon Hong-shim (Nam Ji-hyun) lives.

238-2 Museom-ro, Munsu-myeon, Yeongju-si, Gyeongsangbuk-do

한국민속촌

Hong Shim (Nam Ji-hyun) has been waiting for her older brother at the 'wooden bridge' once a month, a promise they made 10 years ago. This is also the place where Jung Je-yoon (Kim Sun-ho) and adult Lee Yul (EXO's D.O.) ...

90 Minsokchon-ro, Giheung-gu, Yongin-si, Gyeonggi-do

부안영상테마파크

In Episode 2, a banquet hosted by Yi Yul (Do Kyung-soo) to find the assassin of female doctor Song Sun (Kim Chae-an) unfolds at this location.

309-64 Gyeokpo-ro, Banseok-myeon, Buan-gun, Jeollabuk-do
