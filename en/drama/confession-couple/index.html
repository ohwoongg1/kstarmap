All Locations

경복궁

After a date in the sky, Choi Ban-do (Son Ho-jun) says a happy goodbye.

161 Sajik-ro, Jongno-gu, Seoul

하늘공원

In Episode 4, Min-seo-young (Go Bo-gyeol) and Choi Ban-do (Son Ho-joon) came here on a date.

95 Haneulgongwon-ro, Mapo-gu, Seoul

곱창전골

In Episode 2, after completing his time travel, Choi Ban-do (Son Ho-jun) attends and realizes this is where they first met. He does his best to prevent history from repeating itself.

8 Wausan-ro 29-gil, Mapo-gu, Seoul

덕성여자대학교

In the drama, this location serves as the university attended by Choi Ban-do (Son Ho-jun) and Ma Jin-joo (Jang Na-ra). It appears as 'Korea University,' the school they return to after their time travel journey to 1999.

39 Samyang-ro 144-gil, Dobong-gu, Seoul

로데오프라자

In Episode 12 (the final episode), this is the restaurant where Ma Jin-joo (played by Jang Na-ra) and Choi Ban-do (played by Son Ho-jun) dined with their son and parents.

106 Dongil-ro 20-gil, Gwangjin-gu, Seoul

서울중앙지방법원

In Episode 1, Ma Jin-joo (played by Jang Na-ra) and Choi Ban-do (played by Son Ho-jun) are seen leaving the courthouse after their divorce.

157 Seocho Jungang-ro, Seocho-gu, Seoul

신동카페거리

The street where Go-dok-jae's (Lee Yi-kyung) father's restaurant is located.

Sindong-cafe-geori, Sindong, Youngtong-gu, Suwon-si, Gyeonggi-do

카페로쏘

In Episode 2, Choi Ban-do (Son Ho-jun) dumps Ma Jin-joo (Jang Na-ra).

81-12 Samcheong-ro, Jongno-gu, Seoul

퍼스트가든

In Episode 12, the scene where Ma Jin-joo (Jang Na-ra) and Choi Ban-do's (Son Ho-jun) two new doppelgängers fight in the Sky Garden is filmed at this location.

260 Tapsakgol-gil, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do

향꽃

In Episode 3, we go back to 1999 where Jae-woo (Heo Jung-min) and Bo-reum (Han Bo-reum) have their first date at this location. Jae-woo drinks makgeolli until he passes out drunk.

39 Yonsei-ro 7-an-gil, Seodaemun-gu, Seoul
