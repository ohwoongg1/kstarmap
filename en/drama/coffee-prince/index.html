IDEA VILL

The house where Han Yu-joo (Chae Jung-an), a supporting character in the drama, lives.

27 Pyeongchang 16-gil, Jongno-gu, Seoul

루나터치

In Episode 1, Go Eun-chan (Yoon Eun-hye) passes by this café while making a delivery on her scooter.

127 Gyedong-gil, Jongno-gu, Seoul

베라짜노

In Episode 1, this is the place where Choi Han-gyul (Gong Yoo) meets his cousin Choi Han-sung (Lee Sun-gyun) for dinner after returning from the United States, and where Go Eun-chan (Yoon Eun-hye) works part-time.

13-2 Dosan-daero 57-gil, Gangnam-gu, Seoul

북악팔각정

In Episode 7, Go Eun-chan (Yoon Eun-hye) asks Choi Han-kyul (Gong Yoo) to have lunch together.

267 Bugaksan-ro, Jongno-gu, Seoul

산모퉁이

The residence of Choi Han-sung (Lee Sun-gyun) from Coffee Prince.

153 Baekseok-dong-gil, Jongno-gu, Seoul

서강대교

In Episode 2, Go Eun-chan (Yoon Eun-hye) and Choi Han-gyul (Gong Yoo) are shown driving a convertible across this bridge.

Seogang Daegyo, Yeouido-dong, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

서울애니메이션센터

Choi Han-gyul (Gong Yoo) lives on the rooftop of the Seoul Webtoon Museum, which is part of this complex.

130 Sofa-ro, Jung-gu, Seoul

영풍문고 종로본점

In Episode 7, Choi Han-gyul (Gong Yoo) buys a book about coffee for Go Eun-chan (Yoon Eun-hye) at this bookstore.

41 Cheonggyecheon-ro, Jongno-gu, Seoul

율곡로3길

In Episode 3, Go Eun-chan (Yoon Eun-hye) and Choi Han-gyul (Gong Yoo) run down this street, passing the entrance of Deokseong Girls' Middle School.

Yulgok-ro 3-gil, Jongno-gu, Seoul
