137-41 Banpo-dong, Seocho-gu, Seoul

개화동 상사빌라

In Episode 4, Kim Do-ran (Yui) is living here with the Kim family.

39 Gaehwa 10-gil, Gangseo-gu, Seoul

동작대교

After discovering that her parents are not her biological parents, Kim Do-ran (Yui) is kicked out by her 'mother.' In Episode 5, she comes to this bridge and imagines her father standing beside her, talking with her.

Dongjak-daegyo, Ichon-dong, Yongsan-gu, Seoul

디지털미디어시티역

In Episode 16, Kim Mi-ran (Na Hye-mi) and her mother So Yang-ja (Lim Ye-jin) flee from loan sharks through the train station and manage to catch a train at the last moment.

175 Suksaek-ro, Mapo-gu, Seoul

보정동 카페거리

In Episode 7, Wang Yi-yuk (Jung Eun-woo) is dissatisfied with Jang Da-ya's (Yoon Jin-yi) mother's feelings toward him. This is the location where he comforts her and confirms her love.

Bojeong-ro 15-beon-gil, Giheung-gu, Yongin-si, Gyeonggi-do

서강대교

Wang Dae-ryuk (Lee Jang-woo) and Kim Do-ran (Kim Yoo-jin) crossed this bridge in Episode 28 when commuting to work and tasting bulgogi.

Seogang Daegyo, Yeouido-dong, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

서울 평창동 맨션 494-7

Park Geum-byeong (Jung Jae-sun) lives in this mansion. You can see it in Episode 18 where Kim Do-ran (Kim You-jin/Yui) visits her.

215-1 Pyeongchang-gil, Jongno-gu, Seoul

영시티

This building appears as the 'Spring and Food' company where Wang Dae-ryuk (Lee Jang-woo) and Kim Do-ran (Kim Yoo-jin/Uee, former After School member) work.

25 Munrae-ro 28-gil, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

장흥교도소 세트장

In Episode 1, Il (Choi Soo-jong) is incarcerated at this location, and Kim Dong-chul (Lee Doo-il) visits him regularly.

191 Wondo-ri, Jangheung-eup, Jangheung-gun, Jeollanam-do
