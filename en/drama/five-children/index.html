As mentioned in Episode 12, it's also called a Namsan date. It's a place where couples typically lock padlocks together.

청계천

Lee Yeon-tae (Shin Hye-sun) and Kim Sang-min (Sung Hoon) go on a date. In Episode 46, there is a scene where Sang-min brings a lunchbox.

148 Seorin-dong, Jongno-gu, Seoul
