여의도공원

In Episode 1, Gong Soon-ji (Park Joo-hyun) enjoyed a kimbap triangle with ramyeon alongside the PD (Bae Yu-ram).

68 Yeouigongwon-ro, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

익산 교도소 세트장

In Episode 9, Lee Gwang-sik (Park Sang-myeon) is imprisoned at this location.

207 Hamnang-ro, Seongdang-myeon, Iksan-si, Jeollabuk-do

C156

The Halloween party shown in Episode 12, the final episode, takes place at this location.

156 Dongil-ro, Gwangjin-gu, Seoul

로케이션 이음 (구 로얄퀘스트)

In Episode 6, in a fantasy sequence imagined by Kim Moo-young (Choi Jin-hyuk), he imagines what his future self would look like as a wealthy chaebol heir.

48-70 Saeo-ri-ro 286beon-gil, Tanhyeon-myeon, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do
