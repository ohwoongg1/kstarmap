순천드라마촬영장

Young Kim Yoon-hee (Yim Yoon-ah) lives in the 1970s. For example, in Episode 4, you can see her house when Seo In-ha (Jang Geun-suk) apologizes for reading her diary about coughing up blood due to tuberculosis.

24 Biryegol-gil, Suncheon-si, Jeollanam-do

영주 무섬마을

In Episodes 2-3, six college friends Jeong Ha-na (Lim Yoon-ah), Seo Jun (Jang Geun-suk), Lee Sun-ho (Kim Si-hu), Hwang In-sook (Hwang Bo-ra), Kim Chang-mo (Seo In-guk), and Baek Hye-jung (Son Eun-seo) take a day trip to ...

238-2 Museom-ro, Munsu-myeon, Yeongju-si, Gyeongsangbuk-do

제이드가든

The wedding garden of this location appears as the house where Jeong Ha-na (Im Yoon-ah) stayed with her mother.

80 Haetgol-gil, Namsan-myeon, Chuncheon-si, Gangwon-do

가든포에트

This location appears as an organic café called General Doctor, serving as both Seo-joon's (Jang Geun-suk) office and Jeong Ha-na's (Yoona) garden.

12 Wauwisan-ro 14-gil, Mapo-gu, Seoul

계명대학교 대명캠퍼스

In Episode 4, Daemyung Campus appears as 'Korea University,' the place where everyone enters university in the 1970s and where Seo In-ha (played by Jang Geun-suk) falls for Kim Yoon-hee (played by Yoona) in just 3 second...

104 Myeongdeok-ro, Nam-gu, Daegu

그랜드 워커힐 서울

In Episode 10, Seo-joon (Jang Geun-suk) checks into this hotel. When he can no longer reach Jung-ha-na (Yoon-a), he rushes out of the hotel in his pajamas and calls a taxi.

177 Walkerhill-ro, Gwangjin-gu, Seoul

쎄라비음악다방

Seo In-ha (Jang Geun-suk), Lee Dong-wook (Kim Si-ho), Kim Chang-mo (Seo In-guk), Baek Hye-jung (Son Eun-soo), and Hwang In-seok (Hwang Bo-ra) create wonderful memories together in this place.

60 Gukchaeposang-ro 102-gil, Jung-gu, Daegu

켄싱턴호텔 여의도

In Episode 14, we see Kim Yoon-hee (Kim Mi-sook), Seo Joon (Jang Geun-suk), and Jung Ha-na (Yoona from Girls' Generation) meeting not as each other's lovers, but as children of their parents.

16 Gukhoe-daero 76-gil, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

홍대놀이터공원

Seo-joon (Jang Geun-suk) and Jung Ha-na (Yoona) meet and go on a date here in Episode 11.

19-3 Wausan-ro 21-gil, Mapo-gu, Seoul
