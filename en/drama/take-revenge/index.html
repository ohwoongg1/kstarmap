쁘띠프랑스

Episode 9 depicts Hae-ra Kang (Kim Sa-rang) desperately searching for her son, Lee Ga-on (Jung Hyun-joon).

1063 Hoban-ro, Cheongpyeong-myeon, Gapyeong-gun, Gyeonggi-do

여의도공원

In Episode 2, Kang Hae-ra (Kim Sa-rang) walks through this section of the park to meet with a former colleague and discuss the possibility of returning to work.

68 Yeouigongwon-ro, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

타임스퀘어

In Episode 1, the exterior of this shopping mall is where Kang Hae-ra (Kim Sa-rang) held her autograph signing event. Interior scenes were filmed at a different location.

15 Youngjung-ro, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

파라다이스시티

In Episode 6, Kim Sang-gu (Jung Man-sik) invites Cha Min-jun (Yoon Hyun-min) to a private gathering.

186 Yeongjonghaeansnam-ro 321beon-gil, Jung-gu, Incheon

리븐델 게스트하우스

Kim Tae-on's (Yoo Sun) villa. She sends her lover Lee Hun-seok (Jung Wook) here to rest and recuperate.

262-46 Gojae-gil, Cheongpyeong-myeon, Gapyeong-gun, Gyeonggi-do

서울중앙지방법원

In Episode 1, Cha Min-jun (Yoon Hyun-min) clears himself of sexual assault allegations against his secretary in this courtroom.

157 Seocho Jungang-ro, Seocho-gu, Seoul

쌍대포소금구이 본점

In Episode 1, Kim Hyun-sung (Jung Ui-jae) brings his former senior journalist Kang Hae-ra (Kim Sa-rang) to this restaurant to have drinks.

291 Cheongpa-ro, Yongsan-gu, Seoul

아소산 일산본점

In Episode 6, Kim Sang-gu (Jung Man-sik) and Cha Min-jun (Yoon Hyun-min) meet at this restaurant.

128 Jeongbalsan-ro, Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do

지혜의 숲

In Episode 1, Kang Hae-ra (Kim Sa-rang) holds a book signing at this library café.

145 Hoedong-gil, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do

청계산입구

In Episode 1, Kang Hae-ra (Kim Sa-rang) is late for a fan signing at Exit 1 and decides to take the subway instead of taking the van.

179 Cheonggyesan-ro (Underground), Seocho-gu, Seoul

콘래드 서울 호텔

This hotel is where Kim Hyun-sung (Jung Ui-jae) brought Kang Hae-ra (Kim Sa-rang) to fabricate evidence that he committed infidelity with her while under the influence of drugs.

10 Gukjegugeumyung-ro, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

포스코타워송도

In Episode 3, a press conference takes place at this hotel.

165 Convensia-daero, Yeonsu-gu, Incheon
