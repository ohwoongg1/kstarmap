In Episode 1, passing by the café Pascucci, heading toward Daemyeong-gil, the character follows this street while pursuing Kang Min-joo (Bora).

The metasequoia tree-lined path in this park is a special place for Do Hae-gang (Kim Hyun-joo) and Choi Ji-e.

731 Hosu-ro, Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do

pino

In Episode 13, Choi Se-hun (Jung Il-woo) brings Yu I-ryung (Jin Se-yeon) to this restaurant.

72 Aenigol-gil, Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do

노량진역

In Episode 1, a scene unfolds where a character passes by a disabled beggar on an elevated highway connected to this location, flees while evading police, and then realizes the beggar was a fake.

151 Noryangijin-ro, Dongjak-gu, Seoul

누리꿈스퀘어빌딩

In Episode 2, there's a scene where Yoo I-ryeong (Jin Se-yeon) escapes from her agency's headquarters and runs into the outdoor space of this building while being chased.

396 World Cup Buk-ro, Mapo-gu, Seoul
