Yulgok-ro 3-gil, Jongno-gu, Seoul

179 Cheonggyesan-ro (Underground), Seocho-gu, Seoul

청파로

In Episode 3, as the company celebrates its 30th anniversary, Hong Ye-seul (Seo Ji-hye) finds herself still confused and dazed by her ex-boyfriend Lee So-hwan's (Kim Ji-seok) proposal as she walks across a crosswalk.

425-2 Cheongpa-ro, Jung-gu, Seoul

In Episode 6, Hong Ye-seul (Seo Ji-hye), her ex-boyfriend Lee So-hwan (Kim Ji-seok), and Cha Min-hoo (Yoon Gae-sang) who has an unrequited love for her, drink together at this location.

29 Yonsei-ro 2-gil, Seodaemun-gu, Seoul
