All Locations

더힐하우스

A postpartum care center called 'Serenity Postpartum Care Center' where Oh Hyun-jin (Uhm Ji-won) stays after giving birth, featured in Episode 1.

489 Gangnam-ro, Gangha-myeon, Yangpyeong-gun, Gyeonggi-do

구리암사대교

After being discharged from the hospital, Hyun-jin (Um Ji-won) crosses the bridge to the postpartum care center with her husband Kim Do-yoon (Park Yoon).

947-1 Topyeong-dong, Guri-si, Gyeonggi-do

더리버

In Episode 3, Oh Hyun-jin (Uhm Ji-won) and her husband Kim Do-yoon (Yoon Park) leave the postpartum care center and spend the night there, having a meal at this location.

451 Yongsan-dong 6-ga, Yongsan-gu, Seoul

바바라플라워카페

The meeting between Ha Gyeong-hoon (Nam Yoon-soo) and Park Ha-seon (Park Ha-seon) is depicted.

55 Jungang-ro 1261beon-gil, Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do

서강대교

In Episode 1, Kim Do-yoon (played by Yoon Park) receives news from his wife Oh Hyun-jin (played by Um Ji-won) that she is expecting and sets off with his colleagues along this bridge.

Seogang Daegyo, Yeouido-dong, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

일산문화공원

Ha Kyung-hoon (Nam Yun-soo) and Jo Eun-jung (Park Ha-seon) fell outside the cafe where she was in Episode 8.

845 Janghang-dong, Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do

일산문화광장

In Episode 8 (final episode), Ha Kyung-hoon (Nam Yun-soo) and Jo Eun-jung (Park Ha-sun) sit in this park after falling outside the café where they were.

1271-4 Jungang-ro, Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do

필무드

Jo Eun-jeong (Park Ha-seon) visits this place at the invitation of Ha Kyung-hoon (Nam Yoon-soo) to attend a piano recital.

129 Gisan-ro, Gwangtan-myeon, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do
