All Locations

진관사

In Episode 9, this is the place where everyone goes to commemorate the memorial day of Hyun Jin-heon's (Hyun Bin) older brother.

73 Jingwan-gil, Eunpyeong-gu, Seoul

더플라자

The pastry shop where Kim Sam-soon works is featured as a location.

119 Sogong-ro, Jung-gu, Seoul

삼순이 계단

These stairs became famous through the iconic final kiss scene in Episode 16, and were subsequently named after it. Since then, they have regularly appeared as a filming location.

649 Namsangongwon-gil, Jung-gu, Seoul

영풍문고 종로본점

Hun-jin Heon (Hyun Bin), Yu Hee-jin (Jung Ryeo-won), and Hyun Mi-joo (Seo Ji-hee) visited this bookstore together.

41 Cheonggyecheon-ro, Jongno-gu, Seoul

코엑스 아쿠아리움

In Episode 15, Hyun Jin-heon (Hyun Bin) takes Kim Sam-soon (Kim Sun-a) and Hyun Mi-ju (Seo Ji-hee) to this place.

513 Yeongdong-daero, Gangnam-gu, Seoul
