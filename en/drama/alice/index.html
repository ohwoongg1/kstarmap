롯데월드

In Episode 10, Park Jin-gyeom (Joo Won) and Yoon Tae-yi (Kim Hee-sun) visit this theme park for a day outing. It's also the place where the two take photos that they will see in the future, ahead of Jin-gyeom's death.

240 Olympic-ro, Songpa-gu, Seoul

합천 영상 테마파크

In Episode 1, renowned internist Jang Dong-sik (Jang Hyun-sung) is murdered in his home by a time traveler (Park In-soo), and his young daughter witnesses the killing.

757 Hapcheonhosoo-ro, Yongju-myeon, Hapcheon-gun, Gyeongsangnam-do

CGV 명동역 씨네라이브러리

In Episode 4, Choi Won-young discusses the 'butterfly effect' at the scene, with Park Jin-gyeom (Joo Won) in attendance.

123 Toegyero, Jung-gu, Seoul

라베니체 마치에비뉴

In Episode 1, Park Jin-gyeom (Joo Won) rushes to this shopping mall on the day his mother passes away and spots Yoon Tae-yi (Kim Hee-sun). From a distance, he mistakes her for his mother and moves toward her. Just before...

12 Gimpo Hangang 4-ro, Gimpo-si, Gyeonggi-do

로케이션 이음 (구 로얄퀘스트)

In Episode 1, Hong Eun-soo's mother (Oh Yeon-ah) sits on the airplane returning to Korea with tears welling up in her eyes as she gazes at her seat.

48-70 Saeo-ri-ro 286beon-gil, Tanhyeon-myeon, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do

바빌리안테이블

In Episode 10, Kim Do-yeon (Lee Da-in) and her parents come here for a meal.

5-2 Apgujeong-ro 46-gil, Gangnam-gu, Seoul

부띠크모나코

In Episode 2, he comes to this building and discovers a mysterious flying object hovering around the area, and finds the deceased Yang Min-sung (Shin).

397 Seocho-daero, Seocho-gu, Seoul

부암동 7번지

Park Jin-gyeom (Joo Won) and Kim Dong-ho (Lee Jae-yoon) conduct an investigation at this house to confirm whether Seok O-won (Choi Won-young) used it as a hideout.

147 Bugaksan-ro, Jongno-gu, Seoul

서강대교

Park Jin-gyeom (Joo Won) and Yoon Tae-yi (Kim Hee-sun) drive across this bridge.

Seogang Daegyo, Yeouido-dong, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

영화루

This is a Chinese restaurant run by the parents of Yoon Tae-i (Kim Hee-sun).

65 Jahamun-ro 7-gil, Jongno-gu, Seoul

용마랜드

In Episode 10, Park Jin-gyeom (Joo Won) and the police receive a tip about the whereabouts of director Seok O-won and head to this abandoned theme park.

118 Mangwu-ro 70-gil, Jungnang-gu, Seoul

중앙대학교 서울캠퍼스

This university appears as 'Korea University' in the drama. Yoon Tae-yi (Kim Hee-sun) studies as an undergraduate in 2010 and works as a lecturer in 2020.

84 Heukseok-ro, Dongjak-gu, Seoul

지혜의 숲

In Episode 6, a time traveler taps a guest (who appears to be their past self) on the shoulder in the library's bookshelf section.

145 Hoedong-gil, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do

카나

Park Jin-gyeom (Joo Won) and Yoon Tae-i (Kim Hee-sun) meet at this location in Episodes 5-6, where they drink and have a conversation. At this place, Tae-i shares stories about her childhood with Jin-gyeom.

303 Tojeong-ro, Mapo-gu, Seoul

하우스 전주 노송동

In Episode 1, this is the childhood home where Park Jin-gyeom (Cho Seung-woo) lived with his mother Park Seon-yeong (Kim Hee-sun).

37-17 Inbong 1-gil, Wansan-gu, Jeonju-si, Jeollabuk-do
