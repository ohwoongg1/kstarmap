한담해안산책로

Filming location from The Day Destruction Came to Our Front Door

K-DramaAttraction서인국박보영
In Episode 5, Myeolmang (Seo In-guk) becomes puzzled by Dongyeong (Park Bo-young) and observes her, trying to understand her better. After following her around for some time, he realizes she's carrying a heavy burden of sadness and wants to lighten her load. He covers her eyes and guides her to a special beach in Jeju Island, Dongyeong's cherished spot nestled along a coastal promenade. She shares that as a child, she wanted to come here every day to cry, and opens up about her childhood memories. They share an awkward kiss and return home as if nothing happened. Later, in Episodes 10 and 11, as Dongyeong leaves Seoul, missing her family and friends, he finds her at this beach. She finally admits they've broken up and acknowledges that her wish is simply to live. Myeolmang confesses that he also wants to live and wishes to stay by Dongyeong's side until the very end.

Address

1359 Gwakji-ri, Aewol-eup, Jeju-si, Jeju Special Self-Governing Province
