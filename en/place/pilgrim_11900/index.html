김녕해수욕장

Filming location from Would You Like to Have Dinner Together?

K-DramaAttraction서지혜김정현송승헌
In Episode 2, after Woo Do-hee (Seo Ji-hye) discovers that her boyfriend Young-dong (Kim Jung-hyun, cameo) has been unfaithful, he takes her to this beach. Kim Hae-gyeong (Song Seung-heon), who has been following her without her knowledge while planning to end his own life, arrives at the same location. As Do-hee stands on a lava rock very close to the sea and struggles to maintain her balance, Hae-gyeong rushes in to save her. He ends up falling into the sea himself, ultimately suffering a mild concussion.

Address

7-6 Haemaji-haean-ro, Gujwa-eup, Jeju-si, Jeju Special Self-Governing Province
