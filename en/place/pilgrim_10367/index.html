외돌개

Filming location from The Day Destruction Came to Our Front Door

K-DramaAttraction박보영서인국신도현
In Episode 11, days after she runs away to Jeju Island, Dongkyung's family and friends receive a call from Myulmang and arrive to find her. Dongkyung takes a selfie at this landmark observatory with her best friend Jina (Shin Do-hyun) as they walk along a forest trail. She reflects on her escape attempt, saying, "I thought I was running away, but once I got here, I realized that everything I love is right here." Jina promises to always be by her side and cheer her on rather than run away with her. Jina confesses that she's dating Hyungkyou again, but Dongkyung questions her about Cha Ju-ik from work, saying he doesn't seem right for her. Jina denies it's true. Meanwhile, Myulmang watches them from a nearby cliff.

Address

791 Seohong-dong, Seogwipo-si, Jeju-do
