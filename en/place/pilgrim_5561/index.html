한국민속촌

Filming location from 100 Days My Prince

K-DramaAttraction남지현김선호도경수
Hong Shim (Nam Ji-hyun) has been waiting for her older brother at the 'wooden bridge' once a month, a promise they made 10 years ago. This is also the place where Jung Je-yoon (Kim Sun-ho) and adult Lee Yul (EXO's D.O.) first see Hong Shim in Episode 2. In Episode 5, Je-yoon prepares floating lanterns to make his wish come true.

Address

90 Minsokchon-ro, Giheung-gu, Yongin-si, Gyeonggi-do
